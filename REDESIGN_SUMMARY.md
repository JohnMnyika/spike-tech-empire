## 🎓 DESIGN DECISIONS EXPLAINED

### **Why "Build Enterprise Software 10x Faster"?**
- Specific outcome (10x faster, not just "build")
- Benefit-focused (what it does for you)
- Action-oriented (build, not "transform")
- Measurable (10x is concrete)

### **Why Add Trust Badges?**
- Removes objections upfront
- Clear about commitment level
- Shows reliability (99.9% SLA)
- Indicates support quality

### **Why Better Card Design?**
- Premium feel (depth, shadows)
- Better visual interest (hover effects)
- More engaging (animations)
- Better hierarchy (checkmarks vs dots)

### **Why Framer Motion Button?**
- Better feedback (scale animation)
- Premium feel (smooth interactions)
- Better UX (user knows it's clickable)
- Not over-animated (kept subtle)
